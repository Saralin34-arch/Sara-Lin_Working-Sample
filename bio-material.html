Bio-material: Exploring Mycelium and the World Around

Beyond exploring formal iterations and their relationship to building performance and efficiency in architectural design, I’m also interested in materials that enable more sustainable environments. In my undergraduate thesis and during my work in an applied building-science lab, I investigated mycelium—examining its material properties as well as practical pathways for real-world application.
